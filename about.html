MITS Foundation

Fostering Entrepreneurship and Innovation

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) has inculcated the culture of entrepreneurship. MITS initiated the incubation centre in the year 2023 with an intention to provide a creative atmosphere, wherein new ideas and projects can flourish. The incubation centre is registered as Madanapalle Institute of Technology and Science Foundation (MITS Foundation), a section 8 company as per the Companies Act, 2013. The foundation was registered on 3rd October 2024.

Purpose and Services

The Foundation is established to offer a platform for staff, students, alumni, and the general public who are aligned with its theme, for the purpose of business incubation and innovation. The center is taking an active role in fostering an entrepreneurial culture and environment for ideating, establishing, and commercializing businesses. It provides working spaces for incubation and offers counseling services on business management and technological solutions.

Innovation and Commercialization

The approach is intended to help innovators in transforming their ideas into products. It identifies tools, devices, and technology and puts together a network to commercialize the items or services, thereby innovators and entrepreneurs can get benefitted.

Our Objectives

The main objectives that drive our foundation forward

To inspire faculty, staff and students to participate in innovation and product development.

To connect scientific and technology related industries and higher education institution

To establish a platform for information exchange and application in real-time research.

To educate researchers, faculty, and students about patenting.

To transfer technology based entrepreneurial knowledge, methodology, and practice and support for students' creative ideas.
