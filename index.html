MITS Foundation

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a prominent incubation center for developing sustainable and successful startup ventures and contribute to the Socio-techno economic development of the nation.

Mission

MITS Foundation is committed to foster an innovation and entrepreneurship culture and support budding innovators in their early-stage business ventures.

Thrust Areas

Our key thrust areas include:

Education Empowerment

Empowering students and educators through innovative education models, mentorship, and workshops to promote knowledge sharing and technological growth.

Cyber & AI Innovation

Advancing Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Information & Communication Technology (ICT) to create secure digital systems and smarter communication networks.

Drone & Automation

Exploring Drone Technology, Manufacturing, and Automation to solve social and national challenges with advanced robotics and autonomous solutions.

Smart Construction

Innovating with Building Materials and Construction Technology for smarter infrastructure, sustainable designs, and efficient project management.

Renewable Energy

Promoting Renewable Energy solutions like solar and wind technologies to support environmental sustainability and cleaner energy alternatives.

Electric Mobility Evolution

Encouraging Electric Mobility innovations with electric vehicles and charging infrastructure for eco-friendly transportation solutions.
